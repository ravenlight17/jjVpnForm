PC Instructions & MAC Instructions

PC Instructions:

  1. On the bottom left of the desktop in the search bar next to the START button type: About your pc then press Enter
  2. A new window will open. Look next to Device Name. The syntax would be: C##-####.
  3. Congratulations! You have now found your Computer Name on a Windows 10 device.

MAC Instructions:

  1. On the Mac, click the Apple Menu located in the upper left-hand corner of the screen:
  2. Click on System Preferences

  3. Click on Sharing

  4. The computer name for your Mac appears at the top of Sharing preferences.
